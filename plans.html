Understanding Your Options

Tier A or Tier B — same destination.

Both tiers end with you owning the asset outright. The difference is your entry deposit and how long your contract runs. Choose based on what you can put down upfront — nothing else.

Tier A

Higher Deposit. Shorter Contract.

Pay more upfront, complete sooner, and pay slightly less overall. Best for clients who can commit a larger deposit from the start.

Entry depositHigher
Weekly remittanceHigher
Contract durationShorter
Total contract valueSlightly lower

* Full figures per asset in the sections below.

Tier B

Lower Deposit. Longer Contract.

Start with a lower upfront commitment. Your weekly remittance is smaller and your contract runs longer — paying slightly more overall.
