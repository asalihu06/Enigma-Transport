The Process

Simple steps to full ownership.

From your first conversation with us to the day the asset is legally yours — here is what to expect.

1

Choose Your Asset

Pick the asset you want to own and select the plan tier that matches your deposit capacity.

2

Get Verified

We call you to confirm details and walk through the plan. You also present your guarantor at this stage.

3

Pay Deposit & Sign

Why It Works

Built around real earnings.

Most drivers in Abuja earn enough to own their vehicle — they just can't cover the full upfront cost. Enigma bridges that gap with structured hire purchase plans built around how you actually earn: weekly, consistently, and on the road.

  • No hidden fees — your deposit, weekly payment, and total cost are all confirmed before you sign
  • Full ownership transfer — the asset is legally yours the moment your contract is complete
  • Two plan tiers — choose based on what you can put down upfront
  • Weekly remittances — pay from your weekly earnings, no large mid-contract lump sums

No Hidden Fees

Every figure — deposit, weekly remittance, and total contract value — is agreed and documented before you receive your asset.

True Ownership

This is not a rental. Complete your remittances and full legal ownership of the asset transfers to you — permanently.

Two Plan Tiers

Every asset comes in two tiers. Pay more upfront for a shorter contract, or start with a lower deposit and take more time.

Demand-Driven Model

We don't stock idle assets. Once your request is confirmed and deposit received, we source and deploy your asset directly to you.
