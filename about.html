Our Mission

A structured path to
real ownership.

Enigma Transport Services was founded in Abuja in 2026 to address a problem that is both widespread and widely ignored: thousands of transport workers across the city earn a consistent daily income, yet remain locked in a permanent cycle of renting vehicles they will never own. Every naira they pay in rent builds someone else's equity — not theirs.

"We believe that if you work hard enough to earn from a vehicle every day, you should eventually own it."

We offer structured, high-equity hire purchase plans across four asset categories — from wheelbarrows to used automobiles. Every plan has two tiers, fixed weekly remittances, and a legally binding ownership transfer on completion. No bank account required for entry-level assets. No hidden charges. No ambiguity. Just a clear agreement between us and the client, backed by a guarantor and enforced through digital payment tracking.

Our vision is to become Abuja's most trusted asset-ownership platform for transport entrepreneurs — a system where every driver knows exactly what they're signing, exactly what they owe, and exactly when they'll own their asset outright.

Transparency

Every figure is fixed and agreed before you sign. Deposit amount, weekly remittance, total contract value — nothing changes mid-plan, and nothing is hidden in the fine print.

Simplicity

A straightforward application process, a plain-language contract, and a direct path from initial deposit to full ownership. We remove the complexity that keeps most people from ever starting.

🌱

Empowerment

Owning your asset changes your entire relationship with your income. You earn more, answer to no one, and build something that lasts. That shift is what every plan at Enigma is designed to enable.

Accountability

Every plan is protected by a guarantor-backed contract, GPS tracking on motorised assets, and a real-time digital payment ledger. These are not extras — they are the foundation of how we operate.

How We Operate

Assets sourced
on demand.

Enigma does not purchase and hold a standing fleet of vehicles waiting for clients. We operate a demand-driven model — every asset is sourced, vetted, and deployed only after a client's plan is confirmed, the initial deposit is received, and the hire purchase contract is signed by both the client and their guarantor.

This approach keeps our operation lean and disciplined. It also means every client receives an asset that has been specifically sourced and checked for them — not a vehicle that has been sitting in a lot. Quality over volume, at every stage.

Our current active deployment is focused on wheelbarrows — the most accessible asset in our catalogue, with fast turnaround and a low barrier to entry. However, we are fully equipped to source and deploy motorbikes, Keke Napep, and used automobiles within a short turnaround once a client's commitment and deposit are confirmed. If you have a specific asset in mind, reach out and we will confirm availability and timelines directly.

No Idle Assets

Every asset we source is tied to a confirmed client plan with a signed contract and received deposit. We do not speculate on stock or carry idle inventory.

Fast Deployment
